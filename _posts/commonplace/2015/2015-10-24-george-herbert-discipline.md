“Throw away thy rod,
<br>Throw away thy wrath:
<br>     O my God,
<br>Take the gentle path.

For my heart’s desire
<br>Unto thine is bent:
<br>     I aspire
<br>To a full consent.

Not a word or look
<br>I affect to own,
<br>     But by book,
<br>And thy book alone.”
